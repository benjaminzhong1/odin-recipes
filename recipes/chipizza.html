  • Prep-35mins
  • Cook-30mins
  • Additional-15mins
  • Total-1hr 20mins
  • Serving/Yield-12 Muffins
  • 2tsp olive oil
  • 16oz cold pizza dough
  • 4oz provolone cheese (diced)
  • 3/4 cooked crumbled sausage
  • 1/2 cup cooked diced sweet peppers
  • 4oz mozzarella cheese grated
  • 1/2 pizza sauce
  • 3/4 grated parmigiano-Reggiano
  1. preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. roll dough flat ~1/8inch thickness then punch out 12 4 inch circles, rework dough if necessary for more circles
  3. with a muffin tray press each circle into each forming a muffin shape
  4. put your desired ingredients into each muffin, recommended 1 tbsp of provolone, sausage and 2 tspns of peppers, top off each muffin with 1tbsp mozzarella cheese and 2 tbsps pizza sauvce
  5. bake for 30-35 minutes and let rest for 10-15 minutes afterwares
  6. eat and enjoy!
